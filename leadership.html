Acting Chairman

Tarique Rahman is the Acting Chairman of the Bangladesh Nationalist Party (BNP). He continues the political vision of Shaheed President Ziaur Rahman and former Prime Minister Begum Khaleda Zia.

Under his leadership the party focuses on democratic rights, economic development and strengthening political institutions for the people of Bangladesh.
